Frequently Asked Questions

Is the CRM suitable for Indian businesses?
Absolutely! Our CRM is specifically designed for Indian businesses with features like GST-compliant invoicing, WhatsApp integration, and support for multiple Indian languages. It works seamlessly across all network conditions, including 2G/3G areas.
How does the WhatsApp integration work?
Our WhatsApp Business API integration allows you to send automated notifications, appointment reminders, and respond to customer queries directly from the CRM. It's perfect for the Indian market where WhatsApp is widely used for business communication.
Can I access the CRM on mobile?
Yes! Our mobile-responsive web app works on all devices, and we also offer native mobile apps for both Android and iOS. The app is optimized to work even with limited internet connectivity, which is common in many parts of India.
How does the pricing work for Indian businesses?
We offer competitive pricing in Indian Rupees with flexible plans based on the number of users and features required. We also have special pricing for startups and MSMEs under the Make in India initiative. All prices are inclusive of GST.
What kind of support do you provide?
We provide 24/5 customer support via WhatsApp, email, and phone. Our support team is based in India and understands local business practices. We also offer training sessions in Hindi and English to help your team get the most out of our CRM.
